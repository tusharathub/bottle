Our Timeline

1878

The Founding Vision

Glen McKenna was established by Ted Evelyn Mosby, driven by a vision to craft the finest Highland Single Malt, using age-old techniques passed down through generations.

1939

Aged Through Adversity

During the challenging years of the World War, our distillery continued its quiet work, producing exceptional spirits that became a symbol of resilience and enduring quality.

2005

The Global Reach

The new millennium saw a major expansion, blending our traditional methods with cutting-edge innovation, allowing Glen McKenna whiskies to reach enthusiasts worldwide.
